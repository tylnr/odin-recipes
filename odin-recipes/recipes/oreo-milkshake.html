tyler's oreo milkshake recipe

home

this recipe is all you'll need to make the most delicious oreo milkshake you have ever tasted. you want to taste this scrumptious recipe? buy the following ingredients and follow the simple method below!

ingredients:

  • packet of oreos
  • milk
  • vanilla ice cream

method:

  1. get blender
  2. put 6 oreos, 100ml of milk, and 250g of ice cream in blender
  3. blend together
  4. adjust amount of ingredients to reach desired product
  5. voila!! drink and enjoy
